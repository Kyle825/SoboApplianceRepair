Parts & Additional Fees

Parts Policy

Parts + 20% Handling

  • Parts are billed at our cost plus 20% handling (covers sourcing, shipping logistics, and warranty handling).
  • We primarily use new OEM parts whenever possible.
  • 90-day workmanship (labor) warranty on the repair performed.
  • Parts are covered by manufacturer warranty. If we supplied the part, we handle the replacement process.
  • If used/reconditioned parts are installed, they are clearly labeled on your invoice.
  • Parts are subject to applicable Maryland sales tax when itemized separately.
